UX Topia 2020

 

gesture What is this about?

 

UX Topias are community-organized study groups for people to learn how to build Android apps in the Kotlin programming language, using the curriculum provided by Google.

There are two tracks available:
-New to Programming Track - for people who are new to programming, follows the Android Basics in Kotlin course.
-Prior Programming Experience Track - for people who already have programming experience, follows the Android Kotlin Fundamentals course. Also includes a pre-work section using Kotlin Koans from JetBrains for those who are new to Kotlin

Do sign up in order to have a seamless experience for #AndroidStudyJam.

Frequently Asked Questions

What all are the prerequisites?

There are no prerequisites. It’s beginner friendly. Please refer the official android website for system requirements.

Can I join both the tracks?

Yes, You can join both the tracks.

Is the study jam open to all?

The study jam is exclusively for the students of Mar Baselios College of Engineering and Technology.

How long should I wait in order to recieve a welcome mail after the event registration?

We will get back to you within 6 hours.
